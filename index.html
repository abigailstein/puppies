CARE AND FEEDING

Puppies need lots of love and care. This includes a minimum of 20 snuggles and/or belly rubs a day. They also need proper exercise - a run around the park or a nice game of fetch always does the trick. Keeping your puppy groomed is also important to avoid fleas or ticks, and an occasional bath is necessary to clean and unstink them! Puppies need different amounts of food depending on their age and size. Send us a message below or consult your local veterinarian to know what's right for your puppy.
